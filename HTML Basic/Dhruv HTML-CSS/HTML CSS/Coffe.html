BREWING PERFECTION

The Perfect Cup

Follow our guide to unlock the full potential of your Nebula Roast coffee.

1

Measure

Use 2 tablespoons (10-12g) of freshly ground coffee for every 6 ounces (180ml) of water. Adjust to taste.

2

Grind

Grind beans immediately before brewing. For drip coffee, aim for a medium-fine consistency like sea salt.

3

Water

Use fresh, filtered water heated to 195-205°F (90-96°C). Too hot and you'll burn, too cool and you'll under-extract.

4

Bloom

Pour just enough water to saturate the grounds (about twice the weight of the coffee). Wait 30 seconds.

5

Pour

Slowly pour remaining water in circular motions. Total brew time should be 3-4 minutes for optimal extraction.

6

Enjoy

Savor immediately. Our dark roasts develop beautifully as they cool slightly. Notice the evolving flavor profile.
