Dogesh Bhai

"Dogesh bhai" is a playful term, not a real person's name. It's used in online communities, particularly on Instagram, to refer to a dog, often in a humorous or affectionate way. The term is derived from "Dog" + "Dinesh/Dagesh" or similar names, and "bhai" (brother in Hindi). It gained popularity after a viral video of a dog accompanying a boy to his study class, with netizens jokingly calling the dog "Dogesh bhai".
"Dogesh": This is a combination of the word "dog" and a common Indian name like "Dinesh" or "Dagesh".
"Bhai": This Hindi word means "brother" and is often used affectionately to refer to a friend or someone close.
